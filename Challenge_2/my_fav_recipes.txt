Recipe Name: Pancakes
Ingredients: Flour, Eggs, Milk, Sugar, Butter

Recipe Name: Spaghetti Bolognese
Ingredients: Spaghetti, Ground Beef, Tomato Sauce, Onions, Garlic, Olive Oil
Instructions: Cook spaghetti. In a separate pan, sauté onions and garlic, add ground beef, and mix in tomato sauce. Serve over spaghetti.

Recipe Name: Caesar Salad
Ingredients: Lettuce, Croutons, Parmesan, Caesar Dressing, Grilled Chicken
Instructions: Mix lettuce, croutons, Parmesan, and Caesar dressing. Add grilled chicken slices on top.
