Recipe Name: Spaghetti Carbonara
Ingredients: 500g spaghetti, 250g guanciale, 5 eggs, 150g pecorino romano, black pepper
Instructions: Boil the spaghetti. Cook guanciale until crispy. Whisk eggs and pecorino together. Combine all ingredients with pasta off heat.
Cuisine Type: Italian
Taste: savory
Preparation Time: 35 minutes

Recipe Name: Chicken Alfredo Pasta
Ingredients: 500g fettuccine, 2 chicken breasts, 200ml heavy cream, 100g parmesan cheese, 3 garlic cloves, 2 tbsp butter, salt, pepper
Instructions: Cook fettuccine. Sauté chicken in butter, add garlic, then stir in cream and parmesan. Combine with pasta.
Cuisine Type: Italian
Taste: creamy
Preparation Time: 40 minutes

Recipe Name: Greek Salad
Ingredients: 2 cucumbers, 4 tomatoes, 1 red onion, 200g feta cheese, 100g black olives, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp oregano
Instructions: Chop cucumbers, tomatoes, and onion. Mix with feta, olives, olive oil, and oregano.
Cuisine Type: Mediterranean
Taste: fresh
Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Recipe Name: Classic Pancakes
Ingredients: 2 cups all-purpose flour, 2 tbsp sugar, 1 tbsp baking powder, 1/2 tsp salt, 1 1/2 cups milk, 2 eggs, 2 tbsp butter

Recipe Name: Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken
Ingredients: 4 chicken breasts, 2 lemons, 3 tbsp olive oil, 2 garlic cloves, 1 tbsp dried oregano, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp black pepper, 1 tbsp fresh parsley
Instructions: Marinate chicken with lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper for at least 30 minutes. Grill until cooked through. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
Cuisine Type: Mediterranean
Taste: tangy
Preparation Time: 40 minutes


Recipe Name: Classic Spaghetti Bolognese
Ingredients: 400g spaghetti, 500g minced beef, 1 onion, 2 garlic cloves, 1 can diced tomatoes, 3 tbsp tomato paste, 1 tsp dried basil, 1 tsp dried oregano, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp black pepper, 2 tbsp olive oil, Parmesan cheese
Instructions: Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Saut� onion and garlic in olive oil until soft. Add minced beef and cook until browned. Stir in diced tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes. Serve sauce over spaghetti and top with Parmesan cheese.
Cuisine Type: Italian
Taste: savory
Preparation Time: 30 minutes
